Gift.it

Gift.it is the biggest project I worked on for Interapt. It is an application that allows any group to manage charitable transactions within a closed system. Groups can provide assistance through Gift.it as a member to member donation system overseen by a managing body. Individuals can be anonymous or have their name associated with needs and donations. In essence Gift.it is a rapidly deployable mobile charity management system with the ability to be rebranded and published with minimum cost to organizations. On top of coding, I was the lead UX designer for this project. I created personas and a site map, and used them to completely redesign the application. It is astounding the changes that design can make, as you can see in the comparisons below.
